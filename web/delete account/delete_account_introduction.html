CMC-UAE Account Deletion Instructions

To delete your account, please send an email to our admin team at: info.app.cmc@gmail.com

In your email, please include the following information so that we can identify your account:

  • Your registered email address used in the app
  • Your full name or username (as registered in the app)
  • Reason for account deletion (optional)

Once we receive your email, our admin team will process your deletion request and permanently remove your account and all associated data.

Example email content:

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Hello Admin,

Please delete my account with the following details:

Email: user@example.com
Username: JohnDoe

Thank you.
