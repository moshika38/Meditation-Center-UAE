CMC UAE – Account Deletion

Request permanent removal of your account
To delete your account, please send an email to:

info.app.cmc@gmail.com

Please include the following details in your email:

  • Registered email address used in the app
  • Your full name or username
  • Reason for account deletion (optional)

Once received, our admin team will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Sample email:

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Hello Admin,

Please delete my CMC UAE account.

Email: user@example.com
Username: JohnDoe

Thank you.
